About Us

RSK Engineering is a construction-focused engineering company offering end-to-end solutions from structural design to project execution. We specialize in residential, commercial, and industrial buildings.

Led by Eng. Rashid S. Kalu, a registered engineer with over 10 years of experience, our team uses software like Archicad, Revit, AutoCAD, and more which guide us in production of three dimensional images & layout plans. Take note that before any project is put in soft copy, we have to generate conceptual sketches first on paper and this are thoroughly discussed until all aspects of the design are concluded.
