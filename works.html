Tower Heights

Tower Heights is an 5-floor residential building in the heart of Embakasi,Nairobi,Kenya, a peaceful yet well-connected suburb. It offers 1BHK, 2BHK, and 3BHK apartments (45–120 sqm) with modern finishes, balconies, and smart layouts. The ground floor includes a lobby, retail spaces, and parking, while upper floors provide comfortable living spaces. Amenities like 24/7 security, elevators, and fire safety ensure convenience. Its glass and concrete façade combines style and functionality, making it ideal for urban professionals and families seeking a balanced lifestyle.

Mtwapa Creek Apartments

A modern residential development situated along the scenic Mtwapa Creek in Kilifi County. Designed to blend contemporary architecture with coastal influences, the apartments feature spacious balconies, large windows for natural lighting, and open-plan layouts that maximize waterfront views. The development includes high-end finishes, modern amenities, and a serene environment ideal for both long-term living and holiday rentals. My role in this project was as an engineer, where I also collaborated closely with the architect to refine the aesthetic design, ensuring a seamless integration of structural efficiency and architectural elegance.

Vikesh Haria Renovations

Vikesh Haria Renovations transformed a 4-bedroom Nyali villa into a modern coastal home. The project introduced open-plan living areas, premium finishes, and expansive windows to maximize ocean views. Key upgrades included a high-end kitchen, redesigned outdoor spaces, and structural improvements. Blending contemporary design with Swahili influences, the renovated home now offers luxurious comfort near Nyali Beach, shopping centers, and schools.
